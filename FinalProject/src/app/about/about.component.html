About Solar Clothing

Welcome to Solar, your number one source for sustainable fashion. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of eco-friendly apparel, with a focus on style, quality, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2010 by Jane Doe, Solar has come a long way from its beginnings in San Francisco. When Jane first started out, her passion for sustainable fashion drove her to quit her day job, do tons of research, so that Solar can offer you clothing made from 100% recycled materials. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
